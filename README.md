# 🗡️ Monster Tapper

![Monster Tapper Demo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/romeototo/monster-tapper/main/screenshot.gif)

An action-packed idle clicker web game. Defeat monsters, earn gold, upgrade your damage, and face challenging Boss Battles with strict time limits!

👉 **[Play Live Demo Here](https://romeototo.github.io/monster-tapper/)**

## 🎮 Gameplay Mechanics

- **Tap to Attack:** Click the monster to deal damage and collect gold.
- **Auto-Attackers:** Hire heroes (DPS) to attack automatically over time.
- **Boss Stages:** Every 10 stages, face a massive Boss with a 30-second timer. Fail, and you're pushed back!
- **Upgrades & Progression:** Scale your damage infinitely to beat exponential enemy health.
- **Visual Polish:** Floating damage numbers, screen shake on critical hits, and dynamic health bars.

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5 Canvas & DOM Elements**
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (Game Loop, State Management, Object Pooling for text)
- **CSS Animations** (Shake effects, hit flashes, CSS variables)
